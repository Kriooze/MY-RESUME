BOOKS I HAVE READ

  • RICH DAD, POOR DAD by Robert Kiyosaki

    It provided me a different perspective to look at finance and wealth.

  • THE POWER OF NOW by Eckhart Tolle

    A powerful book for people struggling with anxiety or depression. If someone is troubled by their thoughts, this book is highly recommended for them. This book taught me to live in the present moment and plan the future productively without being scared of it.

  • THE PSYCHOLOGY OF MONEY by Morgan Housel

    Another finance related book. This is a great book for financial intelligence and for people who wanna learn about managing their money.

  • ATOMIC HABITS by James Clear

    A good book to learn to how to build good habits and to get rid bad habits. Provides technical knowledge about building habits. It stresses on the idea of improving atleast 1% everyday.
    (Mathematically, improving 1% everyday is 37 times in a year.)

  • INTELLIGENT INVESTOR by Benzamin Graham

    I have only read a few pages. This book teaches about investment. Graham is considered the "father of value investing".

  • LAWS OF HUMAN NATURE by Robert Greene

    A great book to learn how to understand people and accept them as they are. Am currently reading this.

  • PRIDE AND PREJUDICE by Jane Austin

    Am also reading this book right now. Will update you.

  • WHY BHARAT MATTERS by Dr. S. Jaisankar

    • Dr. S. Jaisankar is the current Foreign Minister of India. He has written well about the current geopolitics situation in the world. I have read a few pages.
